About I&I Minerals Trading

Company Overview

I&I Minerals Trading Limited is a Zambian-registered subsidiary of I&I Consulting Co. LLC, a U.S.-based company. Established on 4th July 2025, the company is committed to the ethical and efficient trading of high-value minerals across Africa, the Middle East, Asia, and North America.

From its strategic base in Lusaka, Zambia, I&I Minerals Trading facilitates the international supply of non-ferrous metals and precious minerals, offering partners a trusted platform for mineral sourcing backed by transparent practices and professional integrity.

We value the people and communities we engage with, fostering fair trade and mutual respect across diverse cultural environments.

Mission Statement

"To responsibly source and trade copper cathodes, gold, emeralds, diamonds, and other non-ferrous metals and precious minerals, delivering value to our partners through integrity, efficiency, and a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices."
