About I&I Mineral Trading

I&I Mineral Trading Limited is a Zambian-registered subsidiary of I&I Consulting Co. LLC (USA). We are committed to the ethical and efficient trading of copper cathodes, gold, emeralds, diamonds, and other high-value minerals across Africa, the Middle East, Asia, and North America.

From our strategic base in Lusaka, Zambia, we provide a trusted platform for mineral sourcing, backed by transparent practices, professional integrity, and sustainable trade. We value the communities we engage with, fostering fair trade and mutual respect across cultures.

Mission Statement

"To responsibly source and trade copper cathodes, gold, emeralds, diamonds, and other minerals, delivering value through integrity, efficiency, and a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices."
