About I&I Mineral Trading

I&I Mineral Trading Limited is a Zambian-registered subsidiary of I&I Consulting Co. LLC (USA). We are committed to the ethical and efficient trading of copper cathodes, gold, emeralds, diamonds, and other high-value minerals across Africa, the Middle East, Asia, and North America.

From our strategic base in Lusaka, Zambia, we provide a trusted platform for mineral sourcing, backed by transparent practices, professional integrity, and sustainable trade. We value the communities we engage with, fostering fair trade and mutual respect across cultures.

Our Specialization

Copper Cathode

Our company is actively engaged in the sourcing and trading of high-grade copper cathodes from Zambia and the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), two of Africa’s leading copper-producing nations. Through strategic partnerships with licensed mines and reputable suppliers, we ensure a reliable and consistent supply of copper cathodes that meet London Metal Exchange (LME) quality standards.

Gold

We focus on buying and selling gold from Sub-Saharan Africa, a region renowned for its vast mineral wealth. We collaborate with licensed miners and reputable suppliers to provide responsibly sourced gold to both regional and international markets. By leveraging strong local networks and global market knowledge, the company positions itself as a reliable partner connecting Sub-Saharan Africa’s gold resources with global demand.

Diamonds

Our company specializes in the buying and export of high-quality diamonds, with a primary focus on sourcing from the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) and Angola—two of Africa’s most prominent diamond-producing countries. With access to some of the richest diamond fields in the region, the company supplies both rough and polished stones to international markets.

Emeralds

We serve regional and international gemstone markets with rich green and high-quality emeralds from Zambia's Lufwanyama district of the Copperbelt province, an important emerald-producing corridor in Africa. Through strong partnerships with licensed miners and suppliers, the company ensures a consistent supply of ethically acquired emeralds that meet international standards.

Precious Gemstones

We trade in precious gemstones from the Sub-Saharan region, one of the world’s richest areas for high-value minerals. Apart from Emeralds and Diamonds, we also trade in gemstones such as rubies, sapphires, and tourmalines, sourced through reputable and licensed partners across Zambia, Mozambique, Tanzania, Angola, and the Democratic Republic of Congo.
